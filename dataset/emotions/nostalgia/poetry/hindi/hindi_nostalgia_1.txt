chhaaya mat chhoona man, hoga dukh doona. jeevan mein hain surang sudhiyaan suhaavanee chhaviyon kee chitr-gandh phailee manabhaavanee; tan-sugandh shesh rahee, beet gaee yaaminee, kuntal ke phoolon kee yaad banee chaandanee. bhoolee see ek chhuan banata har jeevit kshan chhaaya mat chhoona man, hoga dukh doona. yash hai ya na vaibhav hai, maan hai na saramaaya; jitana hee dauda too utana hee bharamaaya. prabhuta ka sharan bimb keval mrgatrshna hai, har chandrika mein chhipee ek raat krshna hai. jo hai yathaarth kathin usaka too kar poojan chhaaya mat chhoona man, hoga dukh doona. duvidha hat saahas hai, dikhata hai panth nahin, deh sukhee ho par man ke dukh ka ant nahin. dukh hai na chaand khila sharad-raat aane par, kya hua jo khila phool ras-basant jaane par? jo na mila bhool use kar too bhavishy varan, chhaaya mat chhoona man, hoga dukh doona.
