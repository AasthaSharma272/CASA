krodh kee apanee seema hai aur, krodh kee bhee maryaada hai. sahee samay par kiya krodh, parinaam badalata jaata hai. raaghav ne jab krodh kiya tab, saagar bhay se kaamp utha, svarg padhaare jataayu jab, krodhit ho raavan se yuddh kiya. samay pe krodhit na hone ka, dand bheeshm ne saha bahut. draupadee ka apamaan dekhakar, krodh na aaya unako jab. har baaton ko anadekha kar, chup ho jaana sahee nahee. bigadee baat sahee hotee tab, samay par krodh agar aatee.
